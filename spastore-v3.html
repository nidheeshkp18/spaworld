Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up the table?+
The AGO S28 sets up in under 60 seconds. Unfold the legs, click the auto-lock mechanism, and the table is ready. No tools required. The face cradle and armrest attach with simple clip-on fittings.
Is the PU cover easy to clean between clients?+
Yes — the oil and water-resistant PU surface wipes down instantly with a damp cloth or standard spa surface cleaner. It repels massage oils and does not absorb moisture, maintaining professional hygiene standards between sessions.
Can this table support heavier clients?+
Yes. The AGO S28 is rated to support clients up to 300 lbs (136 kg). The aluminium alloy frame with auto-lock legs distributes weight evenly, providing stable and safe support throughout the treatment.
Does it include the carry bag and face cradle?+
Yes. Every AGO S28 comes complete with a padded carry bag, removable face cradle, and adjustable armrests at no extra cost — everything you need ready to use out of the box.
What is the delivery time to UAE and GCC?+
UAE delivery is free and takes 2–4 business days. GCC countries (KSA, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, Kuwait) typically take 3–7 business days with shipping costs calculated at checkout. Contact us on WhatsApp for urgent or bulk orders.
